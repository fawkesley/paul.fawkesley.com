I'm Paul, a Product Engineer

I love building things with users

I care about human rights, internet freedom, equality.

I work best in small autonomous teams

I've built software for printing machines and high-speed network devices, scraped and analysed data for ScraperWiki, Shelter and Global Witness, built backends for Government Digital Service, created the whole stack for Sea Level Research and kicked off the NHS Transformation.

I'm currently tech lead for Digital Product Research at Co-op and technical advisor at Projects By If.

I have a First in Engineering (Electronics) from Durham University. My CV is on LinkedIn.

I enjoy sharing through public speaking

I've talked publicly about Information Hiding at Breakerfaire, Building With Straw at Barcamp, Developers and User Research at People Before Pixels, Modernising the Shipping Industry at a mySociety Meetup and an Introduction to Public Key Cryptography at Liverpool's Bitcoin and Cryptography meetup.

I enjoy communicating complex ideas to non-technical audiences.
